FAQ

Q: How do I change my current password?

A: If you would like to change your current password, you can do so by clicking here. You will be enter your current password and then choose and confirm your new password.


Q: How do I create a post?

A: Create a post by navigating to 'Post Thread' at the top of the main navigation bar. On the Post Thread page, you will be able to enter a title, post message, and even add an image to your post. Click submit and you will have successfully created your own thread!


Q: Can I reply to another user's thread post?

A: Yes! You can reply to a thread by first clicking on another user's post. This will bring you to a new page where you can view the full post and have the option to reply to the post. Type your message in the box under 'Reply to this post' and click Reply when finished.


Q: How do I view my personal profile page?

A: You can view your personal account page by clicking on your username wherever you see it. This should take you directly to your profile page where you can see your profile picture, posts, friends, and groups.
